Overview

Purpose

This site is to be a portfolio of my sister's sourdough bread and something that can allow her to take commissions to make sourdough bread (or other goodies) for people.

Audience

The audience for this website is family, friends and other people interested in ordering sourdough bread from my sister.

Dynamic elements

I plan on creating a gallery of some of her sourdough endeavours with the ability to search through the images using key words like 'chocolate', found in JavaScript descriptor tags. I also plan on trying to create some sort of order form that can send the information to me or my sister when someone tries to order a loaf of sourdough. The form will have things like a dropdown list of options and different fields to fill out for the order. JavaScript will be used to calculate cost of the order as well as send the order request.
