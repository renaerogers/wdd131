The Art of the Rise

Kitty's Sourdough Bread Journey

What started as a simple hobby about two years ago has blossomed into a full-blown sourdough obsession. Since starting her craft, Kitty has dived deep into the world of wild yeast, mastering the art of transforming flour and water into scrumptious golden masterpieces. Kitty's excitement for the craft is baked into every loaf—a passion that was put to the ultimate test this past Christmas when she successfully managed a massive "bread-athon," hand-crafting over 20 loaves for friends and family without missing a beat. Managing a high-volume bake with grace and precision proved that her kitchen is officially open for business! Browse through her some of her past sourdough creations from her first experimental loaves to the professional masterpieces she makes today.

Beyond the Loaf

Kitty's Other Baking Endeavors

While sourdough is Kitty's primary spark, her love for baking extends far into the world of classic comfort. Explore her broader kitchen adventures, where she experiments with everything from the nostalgic warmth of cinnamon bread to the perfect, golden crunch of homemade breadsticks. Whether she’s stretching dough for a family pizza night or perfecting a sweet swirl for a morning treat, these bakes represent her pride in the process and her joy in learning new techniques. These "side quests" are a huge part of Kitty’s baking identity—showcasing the skill, creativity, and heart she pours into every single tray that leaves her oven.
